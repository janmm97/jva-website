Automation Projects

End-to-end automation pipelines that streamline content creation, deal screening, and event management.

Social Media Content Generator

An automated AI-powered content generation and publishing workflow designed to streamline social media ideation, creation, and organization across multiple content formats.

Operates on a scheduled trigger and leverages multiple AI agents to generate fresh, non-repetitive content ideas across categories like thought leadership, promotional posts, memes, and feature-focused content. The system analyzes previously published posts to maintain consistency while ensuring originality.

Each idea is transformed into fully written posts, engaging meme content, concise quotes, and visual asset prompts. The workflow also manages storage by automatically organizing content into spreadsheets and uploading generated media to cloud storage.

Achieved six-figure impressions on individual posts through improved content consistency, creativity, and production speed.

Deal Flow Checker

An AI-powered deal flow automation system that monitors incoming emails from opportunity sources and intelligently filters them based on predefined acquisition criteria.

Triggered in real-time via email, the workflow uses AI agents to analyze each message, identify how many businesses are being presented, and extract key details such as financial metrics, business type, and relevant links. It evaluates each opportunity against specific investment criteria.

For emails containing multiple opportunities, the system breaks them down and analyzes each business individually. Qualified deals are automatically routed to a communication channel while irrelevant ones are filtered out entirely.

Saved 4–5 hours previously spent reviewing deal emails, allowing the team to stay focused on active deals and higher-value opportunities.

Events Review Automation

An intelligent event monitoring and review automation workflow that streamlines the process of tracking, evaluating, and organizing upcoming events and opportunities.

Automated event curation and reduced manual review time significantly.
