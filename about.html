How I Work

Three steps to every engagement.

01 —

Discovery Call

We start with a focused discovery call to understand your business, current systems, bottlenecks, and goals — whether that's VA support, AI automation, n8n workflows, or a more specialized solution. The goal is simple: find the highest-impact opportunities and determine whether we're a strong fit.

02 —

Planning & Proposal

After the call, I map out the best approach — scope, tools, workflow structure, timeline, and key deliverables. You receive a clear proposal outlining what will be done, how it will be executed, and what results we're working toward. No vague tasks. No guesswork.

03 —

Final Offer & Execution

Once the plan is aligned, we finalize the offer and move into execution — setting up automations, managing operations, building AI-powered workflows, or supporting your day-to-day. Throughout, the focus stays on efficiency, clarity, and measurable improvement.
